About Tesla, Inc.

Tesla, Inc. is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company founded in 2003. The company is known for its innovative electric cars, battery energy storage systems, and solar products. Tesla's mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy through the development and production of electric vehicles and renewable energy solutions.

Tesla's product lineup includes the Model S, Model 3, Model X, and Model Y electric vehicles, as well as the Cybertruck and Roadster. The company also produces energy storage products like the Powerwall, Powerpack, and Megapack, along with solar panels and solar roof tiles.

Tesla has been a pioneer in the electric vehicle industry, pushing the boundaries of technology and design. The company's vehicles are known for their impressive performance, long-range capabilities, and advanced autonomous driving features. Tesla has also built a vast network of Supercharger stations to support its electric vehicles.

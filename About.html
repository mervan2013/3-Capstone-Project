About Tesla, Inc.

Accelerating the world’s transition to sustainable energy through innovation, performance, and clean technology.

Who We Are

Tesla, Inc. is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company founded in 2003. Tesla designs and manufactures electric vehicles, battery energy storage systems, and solar products with the mission to create a sustainable future.

Our Products

Tesla’s lineup includes Model S, Model 3, Model X, Model Y, Cybertruck, and Roadster. Beyond vehicles, Tesla develops Powerwall, Powerpack, Megapack, solar panels, and Solar Roof solutions.

Innovation & Sustainability

Tesla continues to redefine transportation with cutting-edge performance, long-range capabilities, autonomous driving technology, and a global Supercharger network.
